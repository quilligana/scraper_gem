PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
An attractive three bedroom detached bungalow on a beautiful site in a rural location. Located between Ahoghill and Portglenone on the Tully Road, this property is conveniently situated for schools, shops and other amenities and is a short drive from Ballymena town.
Internally the accommodation comprises a kitchen/dining room, lounge, three bedrooms and bathroom. Outside there is a range of out-buildings, spacious yard, front and side lawn and approximately 2 acres of land. Other benefits of this property are the oil fired central heating and pvc double glazed windows.
This is a great opportunity to acquire a lovely home in a countryside location with the added benefit of land surrounding the property.

Entrance porch with fully tiled floor and hard wood front door.
LOUNGE 4.60m(15' 1'' ) x 3.91m(12' 10' ')
Laminate strip wooden floor. Stone surround fireplace with stone hearth and back boiler. Television point.
KITCHEN/DINING ROOM 6.96m(22' 10' ') x 2.74m(9' 0'' )
Spacious open plan kitchen-dining area. Good range of high and low level units. Plumbed for washing machine. Part tiled walls and fully tiled floor in kitchen. Laminate strip wooden floor in dining area.
HALLWAY

BATHROOM 2.49m(8' 2'' ) x 2.36m(7' 9'' ) widest point
Four piece white suite comprising panel bath (with shower attachment), corner shower, pedestal wash hand basin and centre flush wc. Hotpress and storage cupboard with enclosed shaving point. Fully tiled floor and walls.
OUTSIDE
This property sits on an elevated site providing beautiful views over the countryside. To the rear is a spacious yard, several out-buildings, including a washing room (12' 'X16' '), stable and garage (30' 'X16' ') and shed (11' 'X16' '). The property also has a front and side lawn, and approximately 2 acres of land.
