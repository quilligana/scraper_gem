Property Description:

We are delighted to offer for sale, this superb detached family residence located in a quiet exclusive cul de sac development of only twelve homes. This property is situated just off the main Killybegs to Glencolmcille roadway and is within walking distance of Killybegs town centre and its essential amenities.

The accommodation has been finished to an exceptional standard with living accommodation extending to some 2,500 sq. ft approximately. The layout provides a large entrance hall flanked on either side by a spacious sitting room and an open plan kitchen / family room which leads through to a formal dining room / conservatory. As an added bonus there is a generous utility adjacent to the kitchen. The ground floor bedroom is situated to the rear of house which is serviced by a fully tiled shower room.

Upstairs, the three bedrooms and study are accessed off a large landing with two bedrooms benefiting from en-suite facilities and walk-in wardrobes. Completing the accommodation on the first floor is a luxury appointed family bathroom.
