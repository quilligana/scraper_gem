Property Description:

On the Instructions of Padraic Monaghan (KMPG)

Savills is proud to present No 68 Orwell Park, an imposing double fronted period residence of immense charm and character, which is located on one of the most sought after residential roads in Dublin 6.

Beyond the elegant facade of this property lies an equally impressive interior
which extends to c.335sq.m/3,612sq.ft and offers gracious proportions and an abundance of original features synonymous with its era such as decorative plasterwork, impressive fireplaces and high ceilings. While this house will require refurbishment to restore it back to its finest it presents a superb project for buyers looking to create an elegant family home.

The property is further complemented by a superb rear garden, which is approximately 110ft long and also enjoys a southerly orientation. This wonderful garden provides ample scope for an extension (subject to planning permission) while still being able to maintain a generous space for the growing family.

No. 68 commands an enviable position along this prestigious tree lined road, which is conveniently located between Dartry and Rathgar. This address provides unparalleled convenience, with Stephens Green approximately 3 miles away and the villages of Rathgar, Rathmines, and Ranelagh all on the doorstep. It is within easy reach of some of Dublin' s most prestigious schools including Sandford Park, Alexandra College, Gonzaga College, the High School, Kildare Place, Muckross College and St Mary' s.

The nearby Luas stops at Cowper Road and Milltown provide swift and easy access to the city, Dundrum Town Centre and Sandyford. An excellent range of sports and leisure facilities are also within striking distance including Riverview Gym, Milltown Golf Club, Palmerston Park and also Brookfield Tennis Club.
