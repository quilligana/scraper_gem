Property Description:

A spectacular property with an ecclectic mix of traditional and contemporary that merge to make
fabulously unique living space that must be experienced to feel the inner sanctuary of this property. The contemporary design and finish to property provides further character to this already distinctive piece of modern architecture that has been designed to provide a warm inviting living area for its occupants.

Creative use of the individual living areas that can be independant yet blend into the overall property makes the property very versatile for modern living requirements.The stunning spiral staircase is a worthy centrepoint from which the whole property revolves around. The staircase leads you to the original Organ Room which was installed and fitted when the house was built. It was built by Kenneth James a well know organ maker (Today's value €150.000)

The kitchen/dining is on the lower ground, this characteristic dining room is a beautiful space with numerous windows providing fabulous natural light. This room has a large round table with 8 chairs. The kitchen has a spacious breakfast counter that seats 4 persons with new modern units including NEFF appliances oven,hob. microwave, fridge, dishwasher and sink. Off the kitchen is a large utility with double sink and washer/dryer.

The utility leads to the Boiler Room with all new boiler and pumps, plumbing and heating system is all new and are on a pressurised system. Beside this is a wine cellar with large deep freeze and new upright fridge. From this level you can enter the garden.

The living room with it's luxurious limestone fireplace also has doors leading out to the balcony that overlooks the magnificent matured garden. The garden is approximately 1.6 acres. The site is in a wooded setting with lake frontage which has a small marina to have a boat berthed.

The property also has a garage with planning permission to convert same and add on a one bedroom apartment which again adds to the versatility of the property.

This property is on a lovely quiet wooded road beside the Ard na Sidhe Hotel, also on Cargh Lake you have Carrig House. The property s 5 miles from Killorglin and approximately 4 miles from Dooks and Glenbeigh

Features:

  • Beautifully matured gardens overlooking the magnificent Caragh Lake
  • Glass Sunroom brings the outside in whatever the weather.
